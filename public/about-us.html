About Us

Home About Us


Color world Textiles is one of the leading Manufacturer and Exporter of an extensive range of home Textiles items that are designed to spruce up your Dining, Kitchen, Bath & Living rooms as well as offices. The best weavers who have been in the same business for generations, are weaving our products.

 

We manufacture an exclusive range of Handloom Rugs, Cotton Bathmats, Chindi Rugs, Braided rugs, Throws, Bedcovers, Poufs, Cushion Covers & others home furnishing items, which are doing extremely well all over the world.

 

We always pay more attention in each step of production to ensure that our products would meet 100% customer's satisfaction & we have a strong tendency to place our customers in the comfortable position to make the highest profit from us. We are driven by maxim of achieving the desired quality at most competitive prices.
